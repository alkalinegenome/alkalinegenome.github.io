Simply IZZE

A Colorful Campaign

Ad Campaign

Color and its connecting emotional energies were the emphasis in this campaign. Vibrant and harmonizing color schemes attract the eyes of the audience while expressive lines help convey the adjective used in the Simply tag. With its focus on being an organic drink, the tag 'simply' implies purity and honesty.
